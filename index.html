Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026  ·  cryptovault.se

1. Introduction

Welcome to Secure Vault ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Secure Vault app and website at https://www.cryptovault.se.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information: • Account information: name and email address when you register. • Wallet data: wallet names, public addresses, and transaction history that you add to the app. We never collect or store your private keys or seed phrases. • Device information: device type, operating system, and app version for diagnostic purposes. • Usage data: how you interact with the app, to help us improve the experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: • Provide, operate, and maintain the Secure Vault app. • Display your portfolio, wallets, assets, and transaction history. • Send security and transaction notifications. • Process payments via Stripe (we never store card details — all card data is handled by Stripe). • Improve and personalise your experience. • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Cryptocurrency & Private Keys

Secure Vault is a companion app. We never have access to your private keys, seed phrases, or hardware wallet PIN. All signing operations happen on your physical hardware device. We only store public wallet addresses and balance data that you choose to add.

5. Payment Processing

Card payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. We do not store, process, or have access to your card number, CVV, or billing details. Stripe's privacy policy is available at https://stripe.com/privacy.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with: • Service providers: such as Stripe for payment processing and cloud hosting providers, under strict data processing agreements. • Legal requirements: if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account and wallet data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at privacy@cryptovault.se. We will delete your data within 30 days of your request.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including SSL/TLS encryption, secure cloud infrastructure, and access controls to protect your data.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@cryptovault.se.

10. Children's Privacy

Secure Vault is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

12. Contact Us

Secure Vault Website: https://www.cryptovault.se Email: privacy@cryptovault.se
